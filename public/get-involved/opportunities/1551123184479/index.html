Category Director, Art & Culture

Founded in 2008, Airbnb is a global travel community that offers magical end-to-end trips, including where you stay, what you do and the people you meet. Airbnb uniquely leverages technology to economically empower millions of people around the world to unlock and monetize their spaces, passions and talents to become hospitality entrepreneurs. Airbnb’s accommodation marketplace offers access to millions of places to stay in more than 191 countries, from apartments and villas to castles, treehouses and B&Bs. With Experiences, people can see a different side to a destination through unique, handcrafted activities run by locals. All of this is brought together in one easy-to-use and beautifully designed website and app. We are looking to re-invent the way people experience Art and Culture in both travel destinations and in local communities. We want our Art and Culture category to delight our guests by giving them unique access with the world’s most hospitable and knowledgeable hosts. And we want to do that all around the world. This is a big, big mission and we need a passionate and committed leader to revolutionize this sector. Leading a small team, you’ll develop our global strategy and work with Product, Marketing and Operations teams to realize that vision and make it a reality. You’ll work in a fast paced and constantly evolving environment. Above all, you’ll need to be ready to embrace the adventure and be an industry pioneer.

Requirements 

  • 12+ years of relevant global operations experience with at least 5+ years in a leadership role
  • Experience in building a business or organization and taking it from zero to scale
  • Passion and domain knowledge for the Art and Culture category
  • A bold and compassionate leader with a proven ability to grow and develop a team
  • Customer or account acquisition experience and a history of meeting or exceeding related targets
  • Track record of maintaining positive relationships and increasing engagement with customers/clients/partners
  • Skilled at managing internal, cross-functional relationships, projects, and workflows
  • Ability to make thoughtful decisions in real time to provide support and solve problems as they arise 
  • Exemplary work in influencing diverse internal and external groups to follow a certain course of action
  • Ability to travel nationally and international every quarter 

Responsibilities

  • Own the vision and execution of Art & Culture on Airbnb Experiences
  • Design and execute a winning business strategy that has quality as its central goal
  • Motivate and lead a high-performance team,  providing clarity with goals, removing obstacles and setting the team up for success
  • Influence operations, product and marketing organization to ensure programs are executed on a global level
  • Responsible for the measurement, quality and effectiveness of existing Art & Culture Experiences and hosts
  • Collaborate with the management team to develop and implement plans for the operational infrastructure of scalable processes, partnerships and marketing initiatives to accommodate the rapid growth objectives
