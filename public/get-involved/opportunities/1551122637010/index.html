Front End Developer

YWCA Harbor Area & South Bay is hiring for a Front-end Developer Contract Position in San Pedro, CA. The perfect candidate will have strong JavaScript experience developing with Vue.js. This is a contract-to-hire situation.

Requirements

  • Experience developing with advanced front-end Web Technologies including, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, and DOM-based development
  • Experience developing with leading JavaScript frameworks (Backbone, Angular, React, Vue etc..)
  • Experience developing with Node.js
  • Has a solid understanding of build tools like Grunt, Webpack, Gulp, Broccoli or Parcel
  • Exercises rigor in automated testing, code quality, and engineering best practices
  • Experience with front-end optimization techniques (both in file weight and perceived/actual page rendering speed)
  • Clear understanding of object-oriented application building experience
  • A good eye for design and ability to match a designer’s expectations
  • Excellent communication skills and being able to work independently or in a full team
  • Experience with mobile and responsive web development
  • Experience with delivering video content or other rich-media in an HTML5 context

Responsibilities:

  • Use front-end development practices, software design principles, SEO optimizations and UI design and general best practices
  • Develop and maintain performance-optimized, cross-platform markup in CSS, HTML, and JavaScript
  • Take visual mock-ups and deliver optimized implementations using a variety
    of technologies including Vue.js, CSS, HTML, Ajax, JavaScript
  • Establish and maintain front-end application best practices and standards
  • Develop functional prototypes to demonstrate concepts and to enable rapid iterative support for evaluative research
