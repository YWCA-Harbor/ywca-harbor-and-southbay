Racial Justice

The goal is to create greater awareness of racial and social injustices and provide the community with the knowledge to affect negative attitudes and behaviors. In addition, we’re committed in the fight against human trafficking, exploitation and modern-day slavery through partnerships with local organizations and programs.

Our History

The YWCA of the Harbor Area and South Bay, despite many modifications over the years, retains the comfort and charm of Julia Morgan’s original design. After more than one hundred years of continuous use by the YWCA, the building still provides services to women and children in the community as originally intended.
