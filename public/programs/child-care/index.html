Child Care Services

We promote open communication between home and school by providing a safe and nurturing environment for children and parents.

The YWCA Child Development program has teamed with WorldTots LA, a partner of the National Association for the Education of Young Children, where children have the freedom to experiment, question, and deal with their intellectual curiosity at their own pace.

Parent choice is a key element in these programs. All programs allow parents to choose the child care arrangement that best suits the unique needs of the child and family. Parents are free to choose from many child care settings including Family Child Care, After School Programs, Parks and Recreation programs. Even a friend or extended family member can be paid to care for your child.

Children are encouraged to develop inquiring minds, sharpen their curiosity and extend their awareness of the world through direct experiences that are relevant to their personal developmental stage. Our teachers provide guidance and tools of learning for children to set the pace and direction.
